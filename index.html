About


Beryt Oliver has been designing, installing, and maintaining luxury gardens in the San Francisco Bay Area for over 30 years, collaborating with many others. We draw inspiration from art, architecture, and the natural world to create beautiful and sustainable landscapes.

Oliver Landscapes has been designing, installing, and maintaining luxury gardens in the San Francisco Bay Area for over 30 years. We draw our inspiration from art, architecture, and the natural world to create beautiful, sustainable landscapes.

Each garden is unique. We shape a personal expression for its owners, their lifestyle, and connection with nature. By using light, shape, color, scent, and movement, outdoor spaces are created that enhance and blend with the architecture.



“As we walk into a garden, however small, we find ourselves in nature. We fall in love with gardens as children and they bring us joy and pleasure the rest of our lives. A place for solitude or friends, conversation and play, or to see what grew overnight."

-Beryt Oliver
